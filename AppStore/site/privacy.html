Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026 · Audio Spectrum Pro
The short version. Audio Spectrum Pro does all of its measurement work on your device. Audio from your microphone is analyzed in real time and is never recorded or sent anywhere. We do not collect personal data, and there are no accounts, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. The app sends only anonymous, aggregate usage statistics (which features are used) to help us improve it — you can turn this off in Settings at any time.

Who we are

Audio Spectrum Pro (the “App”) is developed and published by Croscor (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how the App handles information.

Information we collect

Anonymous usage statistics only. To learn which features are used and improve the app, it sends anonymous, aggregate events — for example, which measurement screen you open. These carry no identifiers and cannot be traced back to you. The App does not collect your name, email, contacts, location, device identifiers, advertising ID, or your audio, and it does not require an account. You can disable usage statistics in the app's Settings at any time.

Microphone

The App needs access to your microphone to perform its core function — measuring sound. Audio captured by the microphone is processed live, on your device, to power the spectrum analyzer, tuner, oscilloscope, loudness meter and RT60 analysis. This audio is never recorded to a file, stored, or transmitted off your device. When you close the App, the audio stream ends and nothing is retained.

Network use

The App performs all of its audio measurement offline on your device and never sends your audio or any personal data over the network. The only data it sends is the anonymous usage statistics described above, and only while that option is enabled.

Third parties

The App contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs and does not use the advertising identifier (IDFA). The anonymous usage statistics are sent to our own self-hosted analytics (Umami); the data is aggregate, not linked to your identity, and never sold or used to track you across apps or websites.

Website analytics

This policy covers the App. Our marketing website (audiospectrum.croscor.com) uses a self-hosted, cookieless analytics tool (Umami) that counts aggregate page views to help us improve the site. It sets no cookies, does not track you across other sites, and does not collect personal data.

Children

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe to use at any age.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing personal for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You can turn off anonymous usage statistics at any time in the app's Settings, and revoke microphone permission in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and post the revised version here.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email popravkin@croscor.com.
