Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026 · Audio Spectrum Pro
The short version. Audio Spectrum Pro does all of its work on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. Audio from your microphone is analyzed in real time and is never recorded or sent anywhere.

Who we are

Audio Spectrum Pro (the “App”) is developed and published by Croscor (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how the App handles information.

Information we collect

None. The App does not collect personal data. Specifically, the App does not gather your name, email, contacts, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash diagnostics, and it does not require an account.

Microphone

The App needs access to your microphone to perform its core function — measuring sound. Audio captured by the microphone is processed live, on your device, to power the spectrum analyzer, tuner, oscilloscope, loudness meter and RT60 analysis. This audio is never recorded to a file, stored, or transmitted off your device. When you close the App, the audio stream ends and nothing is retained.

Network use

The App performs its measurements entirely offline and does not send your audio or any personal data over the network.

Third parties

The App contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any.

Children

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe to use at any age.

Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. Microphone permission can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and post the revised version here.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email popravkin@croscor.com.
