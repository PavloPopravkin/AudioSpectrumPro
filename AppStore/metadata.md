## English (primary)

**App Name:** `Audio Spectrum Pro`

**Subtitle (28):** `Spectrum, tuner, RT60 & more`

**Keywords (98):** `FFT,equalizer,oscilloscope,sound,engineer,loudness,reverberation,pink,noise,acoustics,RT60,monitor`

**Promotional Text (148):** `A full measurement suite for live sound engineers: spectrum analyzer, tuner, oscilloscope, loudness meter, signal generator and RT60 — on your iPhone.`

**Description:**
```
A professional audio toolkit for live sound engineers.

Audio Spectrum Pro is a complete measurement suite on your iPhone: spectrum analyzer, chromatic tuner, oscilloscope, loudness meter, test-signal generator, and RT60 reverberation time measurement. Everything in one app, with a dark interface built for the stage and the concert hall.

SPECTRUM ANALYZER
Real-time spectrum from 50 Hz to 16 kHz on a logarithmic scale. Automatic peak detection highlights problem frequencies instantly. The "EQ Suggestions" panel tells you which band to cut and by how many decibels — before feedback ever happens.

CHROMATIC TUNER
High-precision pitch detection accurate to hundredths of a cent. Supports guitar, bass, violin, viola, cello, ukulele, mandolin, banjo, and a chromatic mode. Adjustable A4 reference, capo support, and a noise gate to reject bleed from the PA.

OSCILLOSCOPE
Real-time waveform display for signal-integrity checks, phase inspection, and clipping detection. The first thing you reach for when something sounds wrong.

LOUDNESS METER
Simultaneous RMS and true-peak readout with a scrolling history graph (120 frames). Watch your average level and catch peak spikes before they slam your limiters.

TEST-SIGNAL GENERATOR
Pink noise, white noise, a logarithmic sine sweep (20 Hz to 20 kHz), or a fixed tone from 50 Hz to 8 kHz — all played through the speaker while the mic keeps analyzing. Tune PA systems, ring out monitor wedges, and find room resonances without a separate signal generator.

RT60 REVERBERATION ANALYSIS
Tap Start, make an impulse (a clap, a click, or a sine sweep) — the app records the decay, computes T20, and extrapolates RT60. Size up a room's acoustics in seconds and know whether they're working for you or against you.

BUILT FOR THE FIELD
- Mic gain control with the volume buttons — one hand, while the other stays on the console
- Fully dark interface — readable under stage lighting, easy on your night vision
- Landscape orientation — lay it on the meter bridge or a side table
- Three interface languages: English, Russian, Ukrainian

Microphone access is required. A speaker or headphones are needed to play test signals.
```

**What's New (1.0):**
```
First release. Spectrum analyzer, chromatic tuner, oscilloscope, loudness meter, test-signal generator, and RT60 reverberation analysis — in one app for live sound engineers.
```
